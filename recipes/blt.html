Bacon, lettuce, and tomato sandwich

I love BLTs. They are my favorite sandwich to eat in summer. They are best with fresh tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • bread
  • cooked bacon
  • lettuce
  • tomato
  • mayonnaise

Directions

  1. Open bread container and pull out two pieces of bread.
  2. Toast the bread.
  3. Cut the tomato into slices.
  4. Open jar of mayonnaise.
  5. Put knife into jar of mayonnaise and scoop some out.
  6. Spread mayonnaise on one side of a piece of toast.
  7. Repeat for the other piece of toast.
  8. Place the bacon slices on top of a piece of toast with mayonnaise.
  9. Put the lettuce and tomato slices on the bacon.
  10. Top with the other piece of toast.
  11. Eat.
